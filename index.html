About
I trained to price risk before I learned to model it. That order matters.
Explainable AI Risk & InsurTech Human Behaviour Policy Analytics

Most machine learning engineers come to risk modeling from the model outward: pick an algorithm, fit it to the data, ship it. I came the other direction. My foundation is actuarial science, the discipline of pricing uncertainty so precisely that a two percent error becomes a broken promise. Every model I build still answers to that standard.

I currently research intelligent systems as a Research Scholar at iLabAfrica, Strathmore University, where my MSc work develops a financial vulnerability index from Kenya's national housing survey, and I lead client engagements through Vondetta, the AI decision systems firm I founded for insurers, health systems, and government institutions across East Africa.

I do not build models that need a translator. If a client cannot see why a system made a decision, I have not finished the job.
