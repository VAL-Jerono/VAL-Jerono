01   About
I fix broken systems with data, curiosity, and a quiet stubbornness that has never learned when to stop.

I am an actuarial analyst and ML engineer who began studying how to measure uncertainty and ended up building systems that act on it. My work sits at the intersection of behaviour, risk, and machine intelligence.

By day, I research intelligent systems at iLabAfrica, Strathmore University and lead engagements at Vondetta, the AI decision systems firm I founded. By night I write, tinker, break things on purpose, and occasionally rebuild them better.

I also believe deeply in African creativity. In young thinkers across this continent building with courage. In the idea that the most sophisticated AI in the world should be solving the most real problems in the world, and many of those problems are right here.
